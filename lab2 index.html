Why I Support Emperor Edelgard

The Logic Behind my Affiliation of the Adrestian Empire

1. Her ideals synch with mine.

Emperor Edelgard seeks to recreate the nation of Fodlan so that people rise and fall by their own merits rather than either being born a filthy rich noble or forever living their lives a commoner. This is how I view our world. People are not just "naturally meant" for what they do, they worked hard to get to where they are. Though I wouldn't go so far as to wage war against the United States to make this sort of change happen, I do believe in a world where one's fate is not predetermined. If we could possibly exist in such a world, I believe it would provide far more benefits to humanity than the world we currently live in.

2. She's more than ambitious enough to garner my admiration.

Emperor Edelgard is far and away one of the most ambitious characters in all of Fire Emblem: Three Houses. She doesn't let anything stand in her way, and if anyone dares to turn against her, she is very quick to dish out punishment to them. She's a model leader, and I seek to follow her example--to obtain power and change the world, whatever it takes.
